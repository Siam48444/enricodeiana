Crypto

UI DESIGN · MOTION DESIGN · 3D

Design System & Motion design for an hybrid wallet app to store and invest in digital assets while you're on the move.

Client

Upwork: Crypto

Credits

Art Director: Enrico Deiana
Designer: Enrico Deiana

Year

2021

The mission was to create a compelling cryptocurrency app to help novice users to manage, buy and sell digital assets. The client asked for a modern mobile app with animated items and a clear homepage adequate to any type of user.

The personal profile had to show information regarding the user's wallet and offer the chances to customize lists and financial movements. He also wanted to add coin overview screen to see performance and fresh informations all in one place, to have total control over his/her investments.
