Medically

UI DESIGN

Design System and UI design for a healthcare mobile app, to enhance the interaction between patients and healthcare providers across multiple touch points.

Client

Ruben Anaut

Credits

Creative Director: Enrico Deiana
Designers: Enrico Deiana
Web Development: Rosario Peditto

Year

2021

Individual healthcare providers and organizations are investing in healthcare apps development to improve the quality of services and promote self-management behaviors in patients.

The mission was to create an intuitive and pleasing hybrid app that could help people schedule/cancel medical appointments, request prescriptions, have the ability to buy prescription drugs directly on the app, and easily access to the user medical records.
