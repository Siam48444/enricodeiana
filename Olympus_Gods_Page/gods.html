Our ambitious mission with the Olympus Gods project was to join the pure passion for art, tech, and the NFT space, converting in a functional Landing Page the whole experience. Avihoo and the team aim to introduce tattoo art into the virtual world and various domains of future Metaverse experiences and the collection merges art made by Avihoo on human skin, together with high-end tech and 3D art, all revolving around the exciting mythology sphere.
